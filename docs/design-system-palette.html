Design Principles

  • Readability First

    Text-heavy academic interfaces demand excellent typography and generous spacing

  • Information Density

    Balanced data presentation allowing users to see critical information at a glance

  • Timeless Aesthetic

    Classic, professional appearance inspired by traditional academic publishing

  • Trust & Authority

    Visual language that conveys scholarly credibility and institutional gravitas
