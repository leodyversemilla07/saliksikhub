## MODIFIED Requirements
### Requirement: Author Approval
Authors SHALL approve finalized PDFs to move manuscripts to ready-for-publication. Authors SHALL receive email notifications when finalized manuscripts are ready for their approval.

#### Scenario: Approval request email
- WHEN a finalized manuscript is uploaded for author approval
- THEN the author receives an email with subject "Manuscript Ready for Approval: [Title]"
- AND the email includes instructions for reviewing and approving the finalized version
- AND provides a direct link to the approval page in the system

#### Scenario: Approval confirmation email
- WHEN an author approves a finalized manuscript
- THEN they receive a confirmation email with subject "Manuscript Approved: [Title]"
- AND the email confirms the approval and provides information about next publication steps
