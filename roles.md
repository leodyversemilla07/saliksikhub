## **Managing Editor**

-   **Access Level**: Full access/permissions (highest level)
-   **Functions**:
    -   Access to dashboard & statistics/metrics
    -   Overall system administration
    -   Can view all submission statuses and workflow stages

## **Editor-in-Chief (EIC)**

-   **Access Level**: Full access/permissions (highest level)
-   **Functions**:
    -   Access to dashboard & statistics/metrics
    -   Editorial decision making authority
    -   Can view all submission statuses and workflow stages
    -   Overall editorial oversight

## **Associate Editor**

-   **Access Level**: Limited access (compared to Managing Editor and EIC)
-   **Functions**:
    -   Editorial decision making (accept, revise, reject, resubmit)
    -   Compose decision letters that include reviewer feedback
    -   Track manuscript revisions and review rounds
    -   Communication logging within the system

## **Language Editor**

-   **Access Level**: Limited access (specialized role)
-   **Functions**:
    -   Copyediting stage of the workflow
    -   Language and editorial review of accepted manuscripts
    -   Part of the production process

## **Author**

-   **Access Level**: Limited access (most restricted)
-   **Functions**:
    -   Submit manuscripts through step-by-step wizard
    -   Upload metadata and multiple files
    -   Resubmit revised manuscripts (usually 3-4 times)
    -   Upload required documents:
        -   Cover Letter
        -   Unidentifiable Manuscript (for blind review)
        -   Figures and tables
        -   Questionnaire and other attachments
        -   Ethics Clearance
        -   Short bio statement
    -   Complete customizable submission checklist
    -   Respond to reviewer feedback and editor decisions

## **Reviewer**

-   **Access Level**: Limited access (task-specific)
-   **Functions**:
    -   Review assigned manuscripts
    -   Upload review reports and annotated manuscripts
    -   Tracked for performance metrics (review time, acceptance rate, quality)
    -   Managed through reviewer database with expertise keywords
    -   Receive automated reminders and invitations
